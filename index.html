FAQ

What is her specialization?
Isabella's specialization is technically magazine & feature as well as narrative nonfiction; however, she is taking coding and data classes to expand her journalism repertoire.
What newspapers has she worked for and in what position?
Isabella has worked at The Independent Florida Alligator as the digital managing editor, metro editor, criminal justice beat reporter and university news assistant. She has also worked at New Tampa & Wesley Chapel News as a contributing writer, WUFT News as a general assignment reporter and at Atrium Magazine as a staff writer and web development team member.
What are some of her top stories?

1. Johnston Chiropractic Health Center closes doors amid sexual assault claims, protests Source: The Independent Florida Alligator

2. ‘We’re going to lose a generation:’ Pandemic gun violence spike leaves many in anguish Source: The Independent Florida Alligator

3. International students watch restrictions loosen in Gainesville as COVID-19 rages at home Source: The Independent Florida Alligator

What are her plans this summer and after graduation?
Isabella plans to become a crime and courts reporter for a prominent newspaper. Before that, however, she hopes to intern as a breaking news reporter as well as code websites and analyze data at her internship.
